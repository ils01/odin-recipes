Togolese Spaghetti Salad

This salad is super easy to make and you may have all the ingredients you need right in you refrigerator and panty.

What to serve with togolese spaghetti salad?

Togo has some other great dishes you can serve for an authentic meal. Togo Grilled Chicken recipe, Djenkoume which is a tomato cornmeal recipe and for dessert why not serve Keke a wonderful Lime Cake recipe.

If you would like to learn more about the country of Togo, its traditions, food and culture be sure to check out “Our Journey to Togo” there you will also find more authentic and ancestral recipes like this popular Togolese Spaghetti Salad.

Ingredients

For the salad

  • 1 16 0z Spaghetti, Thin
  • 2 medium Tomatoes
  • 1 Beet
  • 4 Carrots
  • 1 Small Onion
  • 10 large Eggs
  • 4 cups Lettuce

For the Dressing

  • 1/2 cup Mayonnaise
  • 2 Tbsp dijon mustard
  • 1 Tbsp vinegar
  • 1/2 cup Olive Oil
  • 1 Tbsp Parsley
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

\
  1. Place the eggs and beet in a sauce pan and cover with water, add a little salt
  2. Cook the eggs for 12 minutes and remove the eggs and run cool water over them. Let the beet cook for another 20 minutes or so until tender. Remove and dice when cool enough to handle
  3. Cook the spaghetti following package instruction, run under cool water and put in refrigerator with a touch of oil so it won't stick together.
  4. Mix together all the dressing ingredients and set aside
  5. In a large bowl place the lettuce and the rest of the salad ingredients.
  6. Pour the dressing over all of it and toss gently. Put it in the refrigerator for about 1 hour Serve with French baguettes.
